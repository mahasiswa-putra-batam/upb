History of Universitas Putera Batam

Universitas Putera Batam (UPB) began its journey as an educational institution focused on computer and language education in Batam. It was first established in 1992 as a basic educational institute, and later developed into an academy before officially becoming a university in 2008 through a decree by the Indonesian Ministry of Education.

Since becoming a university, UPB has grown in size and academic offerings, serving students from Batam and surrounding regions. It now aims to prepare graduates who are not only skilled in their fields but also able to compete in a rapidly changing global era.

Profile of Universitas Putera Batam

Universitas Putera Batam is a private higher education institution located in Batam, in the Riau Islands province of Indonesia. The university is known for its emphasis on integrating information and communication technologies into its programs to prepare competent graduates.

UPB operates from multiple campuses in Batam, offering a range of academic programs from undergraduate (S1) to postgraduate levels in various fields of study.
